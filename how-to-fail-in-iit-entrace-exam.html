HOW I FAIL TO GET INTO IIT ? (My journey of kota)

by Devendra 27 june 2020 3 min read

14 April 2017, The day when I arrived at Kota with a single dream to get into IIT. I had believed in my self so much sure I would crack the JEE exam. And Start My preparations. SO HERE I AM GOING TO MENTION SOMETHINGS, WHICH SURPASSED MY DREAM AND GIVE ME A HOLE IN MY LIFE AND LESS SELF CONFIDENCE ON ME WHICH WORK I'M DOING TILL NOW I HAVE NOT 100 % CONFIDENCE ON ME BUT AFTER TODAY THE DAY 27 JUNE 2020. I FULLY SURE THAT I WOULD GAIN ME THAT CONFIDENCE AND IDEA OF DOING WORK.


So here We start with simple things….

Tomorrow is another day to do your today's work

I always used to this thing in my preparation times that I would do this tomorrow, but that tomorrow never came. Firstly I started this in May, where I thought I do it in June and July later. I thought these topics were also covered one more time, so why not I will learn and practice them there. This mentality leads me towards overburden and so many things that I'm not capable of doing.

Run away form hard problems

when I got into situations like I could not solve my things like numerical in maths, some physics theories, theories in chemistry. I used to give up on those things. This leads me to lower self-confidence on those topics later, and later the list of issues keeps increasing.

Don't give my 100 %

When I was overburdened, I failed to give my efforts to lighten that burden. I always keep busy myself, which gives me pleasure, not tension, so I often went to malls and markets. I Keep giving myself unnecessary tasks, By those tasks, I failed to give my full efforts even I want to give.

Doing Unnecessary things

Tasks like now I go for tea after that I will start my study. Keep giving me breaks so I feel better. I never toil myself to fulfill my dreams. Masturbating in my single room is also the worst part of my journey. 

DEEPAWALI HOLIDAYS

"NEVER WASTE YOUR HOLIDAYS UTILISE IT" I completely waste my 10-12 days at my home, and not a single time I open a page. And also visit various places and keep spending my time. EVEN I NOT REVIEW MY SYLLABUS, WHICH I DONE TILL DEEPAWALI IN KOTA. These leads me into complete darkness, where I barely lost myself. Here comes the major factor overconfidence.

OVERCONFIDENCE

Being a topper of my school. I started to pretend to myself Devendra ho jayega Instead of working to achieve. Overconfidence drives me towards "TU FOD DEGA"  Instead of "FODNE KE LIYE PDNA PDEGA."

I HOPE you understand how I fail in my journey. If you keep doing these types of things in preparation for your jee, you will definitely succeed in failing your jee exam.
